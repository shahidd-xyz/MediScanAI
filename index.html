Core Features
Everything your health
needs in one place

From uploading a lab report to knowing if your new medicine is better than the last — MediScan AI covers every step of your health journey.

🧬
AI Report Analysis
Upload any medical report — blood test, X-ray, MRI, prescription — and our AI gives you a human-readable summary with conditions identified, in your language.
🗺️
Symptom Heatmap
See which diseases or symptoms are spreading in your area in real-time. Color-coded colony-level heatmap helps spot outbreaks early and informs government reports.
💊
Medicine Tracker
Track every medicine you take. The system compares your current vs previous medicines and tells you if your health is improving — backed by your own report data.
🔔
Smart Reminders
Timely medicine reminders at the exact scheduled hour. Also get notified about new government health schemes, free check-up camps, and vaccination drives in your area.
🤖
Multilingual AI Chatbot
Ask health questions in Hindi, English, Russian, Tamil, Bengali — any language. The chatbot understands you and responds in the same language with accurate medical guidance.
🏛️
Government Report Export
Auto-generate area-wise health summary reports that can be sent directly to health authorities. Identify community-level disease patterns and support public health decisions.
☁️
Encrypted Cloud Storage
All your medical documents stored securely in the cloud with AES-256 encryption. Access from any device, any time — only you can read your records.
🌐
Multi-Language Support
The entire platform — dashboard, reports, chatbot, notifications — adapts to 50+ languages. True accessibility for every user across India and beyond.
